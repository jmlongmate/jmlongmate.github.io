Climate information, uncertainty, water

I am a PhD student studying land-atmosphere water fluxes in complex terrain, across multiple scales.

I'm interested in the practical challenges of navigating, characterizing uncertainty, and making use of climate information at decision-relevant scales.

Climate Information

Provide salient, credible, legitimate, and usable climate information at decision-relevant scales.

Uncertainty

Estimate multivariate event probabilities with clear partitioning of aleatory and epistemic uncertainties.

Water fluxes in Complex Terrain

Study land-atmosphere water fluxes in complex terrain: specifically, topographic and species-specific controls on transpiration, and flux sensitivity to model resolution.
